OUR MISSION

The Patriots 3470 is a competitive FTC highschool robotics team based in Brentwood, CA. Currently boasting 30 members, we all work together towards a collective goal: to continually iterate, innovate, and inspire with robotics. With the help of our community and our homeschool, The Patriots have become a formidable team in our league.
